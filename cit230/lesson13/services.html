Services

Receptions

We offer wedding reception accomodations. Some of our services include food preparation, set up, as well as options for decorating.

Food Options

The food options include:

  • Seasonal Salad
  • Filet Mignon with Green Beans
  • Lobster with Mashed Potatoes

Setup/Decorations

Setup and decorations are also offered. If you have your own decorations that is fine as well. To use our setup option, please notify us the approximate amount of guests showing up so we can determine how many tables, chairs, etc. are needed.
