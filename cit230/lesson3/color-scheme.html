Color Scheme

I chose these colors for my weather site because I wanted to take a different approach on colors of nature. A lot of the time, we will automatically think that the colors of "weather" include yellow, blue, and green, but from a photographers perspective, there are bright colors, dark, light, and muted. I wanted my colors to portray that mindset.
