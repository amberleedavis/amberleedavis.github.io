Target Audience

Description

The target audience for this website are photographers who are trying to find good predictions of when the best time would be to shoot. A lot of this is based on weather conditions and how much light is outside. This will be a planning tool so they can be at the right place at the right time to get that perfect shot.

Personas

Name: Jamie, 32, professional photographer, likes shooting landscapes and abstract ideas.
Name: Brandon, 21, has a startup photography business, likes creating portraits outdoors.

Scenarios


Scenario 1: A girl who is taking a photography class at BYU-Idaho and has an assignment due next Tuesday, and wants to know the best time to shoot during the weekend.
Scenario 2: A professional photographer wants to take photos of animals in nature, but needs to know if it will be too dark in the next hour.
Scenario 3: A wedding photographer who has a client that wants a bright and airy feel to their images, so she needs to know when the next sunny day will be.
Scneario 4: A mother who wants to take photos of her kids at their favorite playground, but wants to make sure it isn't raining outside.
Scenario 5: A car dealership needs to take sharp images of their cars, but wants to make sure the lighting isn't too harsh to avoid any glares on the cars.
