Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to be a portfolio of all of the work I have done personally or as assignments. It's to show employers the things I have worked on and the value I bring.

Audience

As for my audience, it will most likely be employers at first while I am searching for an internship. After a while, I'm hoping my portfolio will be used as something for potential clients to look at. I plan to do freelance work in the future.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display

I chose Lora as heading font because it is a nice, bold font that is unique and stands out. It has a retro vibe with I am going for. I love how this vstyle of font is trending right now. It adds a lot of texture to my website.

Paragraph Font: Raleway

Raleway is one of my favorite fonts to use for body copy. It's simple and very easy to read. It also is very modern, which is a look I like to go for. It contrasts well with the retro vibe of the other font.

Content Plan

Within my website, I plan to have a few tabs showcasing my work. The first tab will be a home page, which will just be a page about me, what I represent, what my style is, etc. The next page will be for photography, and I will showcase 10-12 images as a gallery page. The next page will be for graphic design and typography. This is where I will add projects, and then add descriptions of what I am trying to show. The last tab will be for web design, and the websites I have created in the past.
