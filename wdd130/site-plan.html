Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to educate people on white water rafting,as well as advertise my services. Other content that will be added to my website are safety rules that should be implemented while rafting.

Audience

My audience will typically be for adults 18-30, and in college, or with a small family. This audience will help me target the right people because they are usually the ones who come in for rafting.

Typography

Heading Font: Lora

I chose Lora as my heading font because it is a nice contrast to my paragraph font. Since it's a serif, it will compliment the sans serif I have chosen for the body copy. I also chose this font because it's simple and easy to read.

Paragraph Font: Raleway

Raleway is one of my favorite fonts to use for body copy. It's simple and very easy to read. It also is very modern, which is a look I like to go for.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.
